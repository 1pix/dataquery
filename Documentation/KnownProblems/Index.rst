Known problems
--------------

The following problems have been identified:

- RIGHT JOINs will not produce a correct Data Structure (i.e. the orphan
  elements on the right side of the join do not appear).

If you have any issues, please refer to the Tesseract Project web site
(http://www.typo3-tesseract.com/).
You may also post your problems to the TYPO3 English mailing list
(typo3.english), so that others may benefit from the answers too.

For bugs or feature requests, please open an entry in the extension's bug
tracker on Forge (http://forge.typo3.org/projects/extension-dataquery/issues).
